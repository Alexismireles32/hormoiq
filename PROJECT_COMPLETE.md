**Purpose**: Daily readiness based on hormonal balance  
**Inputs**: Latest cortisol, testosterone, DHEA tests (within 24h)  
**Output**: Score 0-100 with breakdown

**Components**:
- **Base Score (60%)**: Hormone optimality
- **Balance Score (25%)**: Ratio harmony
- **Consistency (15%)**: Historical stability

**Accuracy**: ¬±5 points, clinically informed ranges

### BioAge‚Ñ¢
**Purpose**: Hormonal age calculation  
**Inputs**: All tests across 3 hormones, minimum 3 tests/2 days  
**Output**: Biological age with breakdown

**Components**:
- **Level Score (50%)**: Current hormone optimality
- **Ratio Score (30%)**: Hormonal balance
- **Behavior Score (20%)**: Consistency & lifestyle

**Protection**: ¬±15 years from chronological age maximum

**Research**: Reconciled from 4 sources (Claude + ChatGPT deep research)

- **Confidence**: Data reliability score
